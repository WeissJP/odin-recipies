Blackened Chicken

Description

Classic Cajun Blackened Chicken

Ingredients

  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ⅛ teaspoon onion powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground white pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • cooking spray
